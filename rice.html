Evaluating this idea

Reach

High:

  • 1 in 5 households in the UK rent
  • 860,000 households who rent moved in the year 2016 to 2017

Source: English Housing Survey 2016-17

Assumption: a high proportion of households moving need to provide evidence of income when they begin a new tenancy.

Impact

Strong:

Reduces the amount of personal data tenants have to share.

Reduces processing time and cost for landlords and estate agents.

Confidence

Medium:

Some good data backing up assumptions (English Housing Survey), but no user research to validate need or willingness to adopt a solution like this.

Effort

Medium:

Technical infrastructure exists in services like View or share your driving licece information.

There may be policy or legal issues.
